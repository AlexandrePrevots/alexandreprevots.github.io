T-REX

UX / UI design




I participated in the hackathon organized by Toshiba on June 01 and 02, 2018

What is a hackathon ?

Contraction of "hack" and "marathon", the hackathon is an event where several teams meet to develop a digital project (application, website, software ...) in a very limited period (a day, a night or a week -end). A jury made up of business and digital experts chooses and rewards the winners.


With a team composed on site, our mission was to answer in 24h to the following issue : "Imagine the ultimate collaborative work tool in digital signage"

For further informations about the event : http://www.hackathon-toshiba.com/



From a common reflection, we worked on an interface that employees could use on a daily basis.
The idea was to create a kind of corporate forum where employees can seek or write feedback in order to help colleagues, seek solutions and avoid future mistakes.

I mainly worked on the interface design of the application on Adobe XD.

The jury gave us the first price
