About me

I'm graduated with a master's degree in UX design at L'École Multimédia in Paris since 2017.
User experience design has changed my vision of designer digital interfaces and my way of managing projects. The user approach is very important to me in order to bring them the best satisfaction and the best positive experience.

I am currently working as a multimedia designer specializing in UX design. This allows me to work in different fields such as the web, motion design, 3D and print.
