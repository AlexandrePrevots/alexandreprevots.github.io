About me

I'm graduated with a master's degree in UX design at L'École Multimédia in Paris since 2017.
User experience has changed my vision of designing in everyday life and my way of managing projects. The user approach is very important to me in order to bring them the best satisfaction and the best positive experience.

I am currently working as a lead designer in user experience and interface. With my team of 5 designers, I lead how to conjugue the best user/customer experience and the business approach of my company and it will be great to share with you directly by message or call.
