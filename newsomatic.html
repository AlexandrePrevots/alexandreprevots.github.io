News-O-Matic

UX design




During my UX design master's degree, I worked with a real customer "News-O-Matic" who wanted to review their conversion tunnel and internationalize himself.

With my team, we brainstormed a solution using the UX methodology from A to Z.

You can see above the deliverable in pdf.



We used the UX methodology to analyze user needs and provide them with optimal service. The most rewarding part of this project is that we worked with 3 targets (parents, teachers and children) to design an interface for them.

Here is the methodology we've used :

• Audit of the existing

• Benchmark of competitors and services that work

• User tests of the existing with parents and children

• Literature search

• Interviews with parents and teachers

• Survey online

• Personas creation

• Creation of experience maps

• Brainstorming

• Wireframes

• Ideation workshops with the customer

• Interface design

• User tests

• Next steps
